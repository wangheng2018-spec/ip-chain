Why IP-Chain?

Three powerful features that make IP protection effortless and secure.

IP Registration

Register your creative work on the blockchain with a tamper-proof timestamp and cryptographic proof of ownership. Each submission generates a unique SHA-256 hash.

Blockchain Protection

Your intellectual property is secured by Ethereum smart contracts. Immutable records ensure your ownership can never be disputed or altered by any party.
